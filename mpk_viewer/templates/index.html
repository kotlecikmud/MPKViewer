How to Use This Map

Selecting a Line: Click on a bus or tram line from the right sidebar or the 'All Routes' dropdown to view its route and stops.

Viewing Stops: Once a line is selected, its stops will be displayed in the right sidebar. Click on a stop to center the map on it.

Live Vehicle Positions: Vehicles are updated in real-time. The timestamp of the last update is shown in the header.

Map Views: Switch between Street and Satellite views using the buttons in the top right.

Reset View: The 'Reset' button returns the map to its default position and zoom level.
